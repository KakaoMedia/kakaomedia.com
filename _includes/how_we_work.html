HOW WE WORK

1. Define Problem

We believe that great companies solve important problems. That is why first, we must clearly understand and communicate what problem we want to solve. This will be the foundation and guiding light of any product we build together.

2. Validate Customers

At the center of our process is the end-user. Who are we solving the problem for? How can we validate our customer? Throughout our project we will always go back to them and repeatedly ask whether what we are building is solving the problem for them.

3. Design Solution

We work with you to design a Minimal Viable Product that can quickly and cost effectively validate your business model. Here we design the mechanisms that will validate our assumptions and allow for data-driven learning approach. Clear expectations are essential to validate the solution.

4. Make it happen

We build prototypes to be nimble, adaptive and scalable. Our process integrates high engagement with end-users. Using Agile Development Methodology our goal is to get our prototype in the hands of beta-users as quick as possible. Our aim is to start learning as much as possible as early as possible.

6. Iterate and Improve

In order to have a solid business model that creates value and also captures value is not easy, but our strategy is simple. After launch, we listen, learn and make small improvements at a time. We help you constantly learn and get better at delighting your customer.
